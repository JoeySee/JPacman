Requirement 0:
File: Blinky.java
Method: nextAiMove()
Description:
The nextAiMove() method specifies what Blinky should do when there is a larger horizonal or vertical gap between it and
the player, but not what to do when the gaps are the same size.
Assumption:
For testing, we assume that nextAiMove() will cause Blinky to move in the vertical direction if the vertical and
horizontal gaps are the same size.
